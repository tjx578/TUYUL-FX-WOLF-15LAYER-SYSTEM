# VIX-DXY Integration

**Type:** Knowledge reference

## Purpose

This note captures why traders often observe VIX and DXY together when reasoning about macro stress, risk appetite, and USD-sensitive flows.

## Working heuristic

- rising VIX often signals risk-off stress
- rising DXY often signals USD demand or defensive positioning
- simultaneous strength in both can imply broad macro stress rather than isolated pair-specific movement

## Constraint

This is interpretive knowledge for humans. It must not be treated as direct engine logic unless formalized, tested, and promoted into architecture and code.
