# Quantum Reflex

**Type:** Experimental concept

## Intent

Capture advanced, high-abstraction thinking around reflexive or quantum-inspired market interpretation without pretending it is already production-safe.

## Promotion bar

Before anything here can influence live decisions, it must be:

1. translated into measurable inputs
2. benchmarked against existing controls
3. bounded by risk and constitutional authority
4. documented in `architecture/` and implemented in code
